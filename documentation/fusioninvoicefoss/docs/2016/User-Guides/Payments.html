How do I enter a payment?

Payments collected manually can be entered against an invoice from the invoice edit screen.

  1. From the invoice edit screen, press the Other button.
  2. Choose Enter Payment.
  1. The amount will contain the invoice balance by default. Adjust if necessary.
  2. Change the payment date if necessary.
  3. Adjust the payment method if necessary.
  4. Optionally add a note for the payment.
  5. Choose whether or not to email a payment receipt after the payment has been entered.
  6. Press Submit.

How do I enable online payments?

Once online payments have been enabled, your clients can pay their invoices quickly, conveniently and securely.

  1. Press the System icon in the top-right menu bar.
  2. Select System Settings.
  1. Select the Online Payments tab.
  2. To enable payments through PayPal, set Enabled to Yes and enter the required information.
  3. To enable payments through Stripe, set Enabled to Yes and enter the required information.
  4. To enable payments through Mollie, set Enabled to Yes and enter the required information.
  5. Press Save.

How does my client pay their invoice online?

** Note: Online payments must be [enabled](#online-payments) before clients can pay online.

  1. When you email an invoice to the client, the email contains a link the client can click to view the invoice online. This is the "public invoice". The public invoice is where clients may pay their invoice by choosing from the online payment methods you have enabled.
